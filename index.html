Welcome to the Atlanta/Athens Health Economists group. This group is for faculty, graduate students, and researchers working in the Atlanta and Athens areas of Georgia. Our goals are to share resources and exchange ideas on issues related to health economics and policy, health outcomes research, and health services research. We also host social and networking events.

Please see the meetings page for upcoming events. All events are by invitation only. Please contact the organizers for more information.
